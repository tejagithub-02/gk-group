Project Overview

GK Avenue is designed with modern architecture and premium facilities to offer a luxurious living experience. With only 10 exclusive flats, the project ensures privacy, elegance, and comfort in every detail.

  • 100% Vaastu Compliant Homes
  • Spacious 2BHK & 3BHK Units
  • RCC Framed Structure
  • High-quality finishes & fittings

Amenities

Enjoy a lifestyle enriched with thoughtfully designed amenities for every age group.

  • Six Passenger Lift
  • 24×7 Security with CCTV Surveillance
  • Power Backup
  • Intercom Facility
  • Well-ventilated Corridors

Floor Plans

Choose between spacious layouts designed for comfort and modern living:

  • 2 BHK (1040 sq.ft.) – Thoughtfully planned with ample space for a modern family.
  • 3 BHK (1430 sq.ft.) – Spacious rooms, multiple balconies, and functional design.

All units are built to maximize natural light and ventilation while maintaining privacy.

Our Journey

Building Dreams, Shaping Futures

For over 25 years, GK Groups has been a leader in real estate development, creating landmark residential and commercial projects across Bangalore, including GK Fourtune, GK Gateway, and GK Pearl. We work closely with our clients to turn their dreams into reality.

Our journey is defined by a commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. From Manyata Tech Park to Horamavu Agra, every project reflects our dedication to crafting spaces that inspire and endure for generations.

Our Mission

To create exceptional living and working spaces that inspire communities, blending innovation, quality, and sustainability in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

Our Values

Our Leadership Team

G Kishore Naidu

Chairman

As the Chairman of GK Group, G Kishore Naidu brings over 25 years of experience in the construction industry. His visionary leadership has driven the company to new heights, ensuring every project reflects excellence and innovation. Under his guidance, GK Group has completed over 50 major projects, including residential complexes, commercial buildings, and infrastructure developments, earning a reputation for reliability and quality. Kishore Naidu is also deeply committed to sustainable practices, integrating eco-friendly technologies into the company's operations to reduce environmental impact while delivering cutting-edge solutions to clients. His strategic foresight has positioned GK Group as a leader in the industry, with a focus on long-term growth and community development. He has also initiated several training programs to upskill the workforce, ensuring that the company remains at the forefront of technological advancements in construction. Additionally, Kishore Naidu has been recognized with numerous industry awards for his contributions to sustainable development and innovation, and he continues to advocate for policies that promote green building practices across the sector.

GT Kiran Kumar

Director

GT Kiran Kumar, our Director, oversees operations with a keen eye for detail. With a background in civil engineering, he ensures that all projects meet the highest standards of quality and safety. Kiran Kumar has been instrumental in implementing advanced project management techniques, which have significantly improved efficiency and reduced timelines across various projects. His expertise in structural design has led to the successful execution of complex projects, including high-rise buildings and industrial facilities. Additionally, he actively mentors young engineers within the company, fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation at GK Group. Kiran Kumar has also introduced rigorous quality control measures, ensuring that every project adheres to international standards, and his leadership has been pivotal in securing several prestigious awards for the company in the construction sector. He has spearheaded the adoption of Building Information Modeling (BIM) technology, which has enhanced project visualization and coordination, leading to fewer errors and cost savings. His commitment to safety has also resulted in GK Group achieving a zero-accident record on multiple projects.

Guntur Vasudeva Naidu

Director - Sales & Marketing

Guntur Vasudeva Naidu, the Director of Sales & Marketing, has been instrumental in expanding our market presence. His strategic insights and customer-focused approach have built strong relationships with clients across the region. Vasudeva Naidu has successfully led campaigns that increased the company's visibility, resulting in a 30% growth in client acquisitions over the past five years. He has also pioneered partnerships with key stakeholders in the real estate sector, enabling GK Group to secure high-profile projects. Beyond sales, he is passionate about community engagement, spearheading initiatives that support local development and create opportunities for underprivileged groups. His innovative marketing strategies have not only enhanced the brand's reputation but also attracted international clients, broadening the company's global footprint. Vasudeva Naidu's dedication to customer satisfaction has set a benchmark in the industry, making GK Group a preferred partner for major construction projects. He has also implemented digital marketing tools to better understand client needs, resulting in tailored solutions that have strengthened client trust. Additionally, his efforts in organizing industry events and seminars have positioned GK Group as a thought leader in the construction and real estate sectors.
